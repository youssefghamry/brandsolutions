1. How does ChefsKing handle payments?

ChefsKing supports both commission-based and subscription-based payment models. Admins can earn through commissions on restaurant sales and delivery charges, or receive recurring subscription fees from restaurants.

2. Is there a mobile app for ChefsKing?

Yes, ChefsKing provides mobile app source code that is compatible with both Android and iOS platforms, allowing users to manage their food delivery business on the go.

3. What security measures does ChefsKing implement to protect user data?

ChefsKing ensures data security through two-factor authentication, and regular penetration testing. It also uses secure data backups to safeguard user information.

4. Can I integrate unlimited languages & payment methods as per my choice?

Yes, there are no limitations to integrating languages & payment methods.
