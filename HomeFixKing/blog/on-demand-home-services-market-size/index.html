On-Demand Home Services Market Size – Insights & Analysis

By Fatema Jahan

Updated - Feb 01, 2025

Updated - February 1, 2025 by Fatema Jahan

The on-demand home services market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 16.7% through 2030. This substantial growth reflects the growing consumer preference for accessible, on-demand services.

In this blog, well explore the on demand home services market size and key factors driving its expansion. After reading this, businessmen will be empowered to recognize opportunities, make informed decisions, and strategically position themselves for success in the market.

What is On Demand Home Service?

On-demand home service refers to a service model that allows customers to request and receive home-related services instantly through digital platforms (such as mobile apps or websites). 

These services can range from cleaning and repairs to maintenance and installations. They are delivered by professionals who respond to customer requests as needed by providing flexibility and convenience. 

The primary characteristic of on-demand home services is their accessibility and speed, which offer customers immediate solutions to their household needs.

What Types of Services are Included in This Market?

The on-demand home services market includes a wide variety of services that meet everyday household needs. This market provides convenient solutions to make home management simpler. Let’s take a quick look at the key services offered in this growing sector.

  • Home Improvement
  • Cleaning Services
  • Handyman Services
  • Moving Services
  • Plumbing Services
  • Electrical Services
  • Landscaping & Gardening Services
  • Pest Control Services
  • Beauty & Wellness Services

Global Growth of On-Demand Home Services Market Size

The global market of online on-demand home services has been experiencing significant growth and is projected to continue expanding in the coming years. In 2024, its market value was estimated at $5.24 billion and is projected to reach $5.95 billion in 2025, reflecting a 13.5% CAGR. By 2032, the market is expected to surge to $21 billion. The North American market is expected to reach $6.17 billion by 2030, with a steady annual growth rate of 15.6%.

This growth is driven by factors such as the increasing penetration of the internet, the widespread use of smartphones, and the rising consumer demand for home services. The COVID-19 pandemic has also contributed to the surge in demand.

In terms of service types, home repair services dominated the market, holding a significant share of 25% in 2023. This includes essential services such as plumbing, electrical repairs, HVAC repairs, and general home maintenance, which are often required urgently.

The market is segmented by platform into web and mobile applications, with mobile platforms gaining significant traction due to the convenience and accessibility they offer to users.

Overall, The online on-demand home services market is set to grow rapidly. Technology, changing consumer habits, and the need for easy home management are driving this growth.

How Did The On-Demand Home Services Market Evolve?

The on-demand home services market is evolving at a quick pace due to several key factors:

Convenience and Accessibility

The growing adoption of digital platforms has made it easier than ever for customers to access home services.

Users can book services like cleaning, plumbing, or repairs in just a few clicks. They can choose their preferred providers and set schedules. This ease of access has increased demand for on-demand home services.

Rising Urbanization

As urban areas expand and populations grow, the demand for professional home services has increased. Urban residents, often busy with work and other commitments, rely heavily on these services to manage household tasks efficiently.

Improved Service Quality and Trust

Online platforms have introduced transparency into the market by allowing customers to review and rate home service providers. 

This system builds trust and ensures better service quality, as businesses prioritize customer satisfaction to maintain their ratings. As a result, more consumers are willing to use on-demand platforms for their home service needs.

Changing Consumer Preferences

Modern consumers prioritize convenience, speed, and customization. They prefer on-demand platforms over traditional models. There is also a growing demand for tailored solutions that fit individual schedules and preferences. 

Advancements in Technology

Technology plays a major role in the growth of the on-demand home services market. Features like mobile apps, artificial intelligence, and automated scheduling have streamlined the booking and delivery process.

GPS tracking and real-time updates enhance the customer experience, while digital payment options make transactions seamless. These advancements attract both consumers and service providers, driving the market growth of home services.

Regional Breakdown Of The On-Demand Home Service Industry

Understanding the regional market dynamics and on-demand home services industry statistics is essential for businesses and investors seeking to enter this rapidly expanding sector. Here’s a breakdown of how the market is evolving across key regions.

North America

North America dominates the global on-demand home services market. With a market share of 38%, North America is the leading region, worth approximately USD 1.63 billion. Urbanization, widespread internet use, and a demand for convenience fuel this growth.

Europe

Europe’s on-demand home services market is growing at a fast pace, with a projected revenue of USD 4,555.3 million by 2030. 

The market is expected to expand at a CAGR of 17% from 2023 to 2030. Key countries driving this growth include the UK, Germany, and France. 

Asia-Pacific

The Asia-Pacific region is witnessing strong growth in the on-demand home services market, with a projected revenue of USD 4,797.8 million by 2030. According to market research, this sector is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 19.2% from 2023 to 2030. 

Key contributors to this growth include countries like India, China, and Japan.

Latin America

The on-demand home services market in Latin America is growing quickly, with an expected revenue of USD 842.8 million by 2030. The market is set to grow at a CAGR of 15.1% from 2023 to 2030. 

This growth is driven by factors like urbanization, a larger middle class, and more people using the Internet in countries such as Brazil and Mexico.

Middle East & Africa

The online on-demand home services market in the Middle East & Africa is growing rapidly, with a projected revenue of USD 214.4 million by 2030. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 18.1% from 2023 to 2030. 

Who Are The Key Market Players In The On-Demand Home Services Industry?

As the demand for on-demand home services continues to rise, several companies are leading the charge. These key players are shaping the market by offering a wide range of services. Let’s explore the companies that are at the forefront of this transformation.

TaskRabbit

Whether it’s cleaning, repairs, or other services, TaskRabbit simplifies the process of finding help around the home.

Users post tasks on the app and choose Taskers based on reviews and prices. Taskers are background-checked to ensure safety and quality.

The app manages scheduling, communication, and payments. It is easy to use and helps users save time. TaskRabbit now operates in many countries, providing reliable help for everyday needs.

Also Read: TaskRabbit vs Handy: Which is the Better Option?

Thumbtack

Thumbtack is a platform that helps people find local professionals for various services. It offers categories like home improvement, event planning, and personal services. Users describe their needs, and Thumbtack matches them with skilled professionals nearby.

Service providers create profiles to showcase their work and get leads from customers. They pay a fee for each lead they receive. Thumbtack focuses on trust and reliability by offering background checks.

Also Read: TaskRabbit vs Thumbtack: Which Platform is Right for You?

Handy

Handy is a digital service that connects individuals with skilled professionals for home-related tasks. It links users to cleaners, handymen, and other specialists, making it easy to book household services.

Customers can book services easily through the website or app. They have the flexibility to choose a time that works for them.

The platform carefully vets professionals to ensure they are qualified and trustworthy. Payments are processed securely online. Handy offers a satisfaction guarantee for peace of mind. It works on a commission basis and takes a portion of the payment users provide for services.

Angi Inc.

Angi Inc. is a digital marketplace that bridges the gap between homeowners and local experts by offering various home services. 

Whether it’s plumbing, electrical repairs, or cleaning, Angi makes it easy for users to find reliable professionals in their area. The platform offers a seamless experience that allows customers to book services directly through its website or app.

The platform offers tools like cost estimates to help users plan their projects. Customers can hire professionals, schedule services, and make payments all in one place.

Angi also operates internationally, with home service marketplaces in various countries. In 2021, the company changed its name from ANGI Homeservices Inc. to Angi Inc.

MyClean

MyClean makes finding cleaning services easier for busy people. It provides cleaning for both homes and businesses. Customers can quickly book services online, get upfront pricing, and pay securely through the website.

The company focuses on delivering high-quality service with a 200% satisfaction guarantee. MyClean also offers additional options like post-construction cleaning and handyman work. It supports its employees with fair practices while meeting the needs of customers with flexible and reliable solutions.

Sheba.xyz

Sheba.xyz, founded in Bangladesh, is a top online platform connecting people with various home and office services. Users can book services, such as cleaning, repairs, and personal care, through their website or app. The platform ensures customer safety by carefully screening service providers for quality.

Sheba.xyz is committed to helping small businesses by offering a space for micro-entrepreneurs to grow. With investments from major partners like Grameenphone, the company is expanding quickly. It aims to reshape how services are delivered in cities and plans to expand across Southeast Asia.

Hello Task

Hello Task is an online platform designed to connect people with skilled professionals for home services like cleaning, repairs, and maintenance. 

The platform makes it easy to book services through its simple app or website, where users can request help and get instant price estimates.

The service ensures quality by checking the credentials of providers and offering reviews from other users. Hello Task focuses on making the process smooth and hassle-free for customers. At the same time, it helps professionals expand their businesses in the growing gig economy.

Urban Company

Urban Company, initially known as UrbanClap, is a leading platform in India for on-demand home services. It brings together customers and trained professionals for services such as cleaning, beauty treatments, plumbing, and carpentry. 

The platform ensures quality by thoroughly screening and training service providers. Customers can book services easily through the app or website, with clear pricing and a satisfaction guarantee. With a growing presence in major cities across India and international locations like Dubai and Australia, Urban Company focuses on providing reliable services while supporting professionals with technology and resources.

Porch

Launched in 2012, Porch is a leading online platform that helps homeowners manage home projects efficiently. It offers a vast directory of professionals skilled in home improvement tasks such as repairs, maintenance, and renovations. 

With user-friendly features like cost guides and expert advice, Porch simplifies the process of finding the right service provider. The platform connects users with a network of over 300,000 home service professionals in the U.S. home services market.

Porch collaborates with major brands like Lowe’s and Wayfair to enhance its services, which makes it a reliable hub for all home-related needs.

The on-demand home services market offers significant opportunities for businesses to grow and innovate. 

Companies can expand their offerings by including niche services such as pet grooming, elderly care, or eco-friendly solutions to serve diverse customer needs. Emerging markets in regions like Southeast Asia and Latin America present growth potential due to increasing urbanization and smartphone adoption.

Businesses can use technology to enhance user experiences through AI-driven features and smart scheduling. Subscription plans for recurring services provide stable revenue, while partnerships with local providers ensure scalability.

With rising demand and evolving consumer expectations, this market holds the potential for long-term success and profitability for forward-thinking businesses.
