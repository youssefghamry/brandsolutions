HomeFixKings smart bidding system allows cleaning professionals to place competitive bids for customers service requests. This way customers always get the best value, while providers can adjust their pricing based on demand.

Customized Requests through On Demand Cleaning Service App

HomeFixKing on demand cleaning service app offers a customized requests feature, where customers can add detailed service specifications. It lets providers offer more personalized and improved services to meet customers expectations ultimately resulting in higher customer satisfaction.

Manage Promotion with Cleaning Services Marketplace Software

Running promotions is a great way to engage customers, especially in the competitive cleaning industry. With HomeFixKings promotion management tools, you can easily set up flash sales, campaigns, discount codes, coupon codes, wallet bonuses, and advertisements. The ability to quickly launch and manage promotions helps retain customers and increase sales during slower periods.

Cross-Platform Software for Cleaning Services

Customers want the flexibility to book cleaning services from any device. HomeFixKing is built with cross-platform compatibility to ensure seamless performance across web, mobile & tablets.

Whether a customer wants to book a service from their desktop or through their smartphone, the experience remains smooth & intuitive. Manage Multi-Zone with Cleaning Services Marketplace Platform

If your cleaning services marketplace covers multiple regions or cities, managing service zones can become complicated. HomeFixKing simplifies this with its multi-zone service management feature.
