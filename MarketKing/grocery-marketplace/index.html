Order Confirmation Model for Groceries

Never miss an order with MarketKing’s two robust order confirmation models. The two models used in this system include a safety netfor well organized order fulfillment.

In the store confirmation model, the vendor confirms the order, prepares the package, and hands it off for delivery – so all the vendors of your marketplace are in maximum control.

In the delivery confirmation model, the delivery partner first accepts the order, then the vendor prepares the package & forwards it to the deliveryman to complete the delivery.

If confirmations are delayed, the admin can step in & assign orders manually, ensuring no order is left unattended leaving you assured of a satisfied customer as well as confirm revenue.
