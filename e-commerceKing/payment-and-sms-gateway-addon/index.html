Payment & SMS Gateway FAQs

1. What is the benefit of adding brandsolutions Payment & SMS Gateway?

Integrating this addon into your e-commerceKing system gives you instant access to the top payment and SMS gateways and helps your business transactions.

2. Can I use the existing payment & SMS gateways of e-commerceKing?

No, when you activate the payment & SMS gateway, the default payment & SMS gateways with the e-commerceKing system become disabled. But youll find all the default gateways inside this addon package. So, no need to worry!

3. Can I remove this payment & SMS gateway anytime?

Yes, you can remove it anytime from the System Addon page of the e-commerceKing admin panel. Once it's removed, your default payment & SMS gateways will be reverted.

4. Is it compatible with any eCommerce project?

It is only compatible with brandsolutions-built products, such as e-commerceKing and others.
